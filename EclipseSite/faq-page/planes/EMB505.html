Phenom 300

By: Robbi WannaSnack


Plane ID: EMB505
List Price: $1,758 per hour
Manufacturer: Embraer
Length of Plane: 51.2ft
Span: 52.2 ft
Passengers: 7-9
Engine: Two PW535E

Description:

The Phenom 300 is a twin-engined cantilever monoplane with a low-positioned, swept wings. It has a horizontal stabiliser in a T-tail configuration.The cabin has room for nine passengers and a two-pilot crew; during single-pilot operation an additional passenger can be carried.
