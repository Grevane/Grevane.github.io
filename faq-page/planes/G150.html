Description:

The G150 is a medium sized private jet, production is ending during the third-quarter of 2017. The G150 is an improved version of G100 with wider and longer cabin, a revised nose and uprated (19.7 kilonewtons (4,400 lbf) engines. Nearly 120 were in service in 2016.
